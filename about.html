About

Moti – storyteller playing with photography, performance, and languages.

"Where are you from?" it's the first thing people want to know..
I'm from my mothers whoom.. (Ha Ha Ha) "but where did you born?" they insist..
I was born in a hospital.. (Ha Ha Ha.. me too..) where was the hospital ?
i was one day old - i can't remember.. but i do remember that:
I moved across Europe and Asia, working in hostels, agricultural farms, and performing on the streets.
I spent a decade in Poland without documents, a period I call “My Sweet jail”.
Instead of asking “Where are you from?”, I became interested in “Who are you?”.

The camera opened a new dimension in my life.
From standing in the spotlights as a performer, I suddenly found myself behind the lens, documenting reality through my own point of view.
The streets remained my favorite stage, and I developed a passion for capturing small conceptual collisions between people, objects, and symbols.

My images often explore contrasts: wealth and poverty, nature and urban environments, humor and discomfort, beauty and decay.
Technical perfection is secondary to me. The story inside the image is what matters most.

MotoSynteza is an attempt to
“make the usual unusual”
